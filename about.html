About Zack Wagithomo

Zack Wagithomo is a dedicated Inspection Officer at Super Metro Bus in Nairobi, Kenya. With a passion for road safety, efficiency, and passenger satisfaction, he ensures that all buses meet the highest standards of operation.

His role involves conducting thorough vehicle inspections, enforcing safety regulations, and ensuring a smooth experience for commuters. Known for his attention to detail and commitment to excellence, Zack plays a crucial role in maintaining the reliability of Super Metro Bus services.
